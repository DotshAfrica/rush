Rush Features

We recognize that customer’s best understand their problems. Rush is tailored to enable customers to easily create their applications by providing a platform they can easily manipulate.

Reports and Analytics

A customizable analytics and reporting module that provides dashboards such as: performance management, stock availability, trends, etc.

Inventory and Order management

Provides management and visibility of stock including stock levels, orders, stock movement in multiple distribution or collection points.

Team Collaboration

Rush provides an in-app collaboration module that helps teams collaborate via messaging, voice & video, and selected email API to messaging.

Task Allocation and monitoring

Allows management of activities conducted on the field like easy creation, editing, allocation, monitoring & approval of tasks as per the organization’s processes.

Route optimization

Provides custom and optimized routes with navigation to field officers and delivery teams that saves on time, improves productivity, and ensures efficiency. This information will be visible to the organization providing real-time data of field activities.

Geo-fencing and Geotagging

Geo-fencing is a location-based service that is used to engage users by providing location-based features such as locational-based authentication and data collection.

Digital authentication

Use digital signatures such as photos, thumbprint to provide proof of completion.

On premise / on cloud & Security

The solution can be provided as SaaS and on-premise depending on the organizational requirements.

Computer Vision

Using Artificial Intelligence to interpret and understand the visual world. Using photos, videos, and deep learning models we can provide accurate usable and actionable data for your organization.

Integration

Our application is API centric; we use API’s for internal module of communication and communicating with a variety of standard external APIs and Equipment.
